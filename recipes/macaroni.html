Macaroni recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 4 carrots, shredded
  • 1 large red onion, chopped
  • ½ green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
  • ½ red bell pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 2 cups mayonnaise
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup white vinegar
  • salt and pepper to taste

This sweet macaroni salad is, by far, the best I've ever put into my mouth.

It has a perfect blend of sweet and tart and is so pretty, thanks to numerous multi-colored veggies.
